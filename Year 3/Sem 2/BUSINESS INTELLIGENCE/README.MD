# 📊 Business Intelligence

## Course Overview

This course focuses on transforming raw data into meaningful insights through data preparation, data modeling, analytics, visualization, and dashboard development. The course emphasizes the use of Business Intelligence tools to support data-driven decision-making and solve real-world business problems.

Throughout the semester, I gained practical experience in data preparation, predictive analytics, and dashboard development using Alteryx and Power BI.

## 💡 Reflection

Business Intelligence is one of the most practical and valuable courses in my Data Engineering programme because it demonstrated how data can be transformed into actionable business insights.

Through laboratory exercises, I learned various analytical techniques such as classification, regression, forecasting, clustering, and principal component analysis. These exercises helped me understand how analytical models can support business decision-making.

The project provided hands-on experience in the complete Business Intelligence workflow, from data preparation and transformation to dashboard development and insight generation. Working with Alteryx and Power BI improved my ability to communicate data effectively through visualizations and storytelling.

One of the most important lessons I learned is that successful analytics is not only about processing data but also about presenting insights in a way that helps stakeholders make informed decisions. This course strengthened my analytical thinking, problem-solving skills, and ability to convert complex data into meaningful business recommendations.

Overall, this course significantly enhanced my data analytics and visualization capabilities and further reinforced my interest in data engineering and business analytics.
