
# 🤖 Tutorial 4 - AI in Data Pipeline

## 📖 Overview

This tutorial explores the integration of Artificial Intelligence into modern data engineering workflows through the development of an AI-assisted data quality pipeline.

The objective was to demonstrate how AI technologies can be combined with workflow orchestration tools to automate data quality assessment, identify potential issues, and improve the efficiency of data processing pipelines.

Through this tutorial, I gained hands-on experience with AI-assisted data engineering, workflow automation, and intelligent data quality management.

This tutorial was one of the most interesting and relevant activities throughout the course because it combined two important areas of modern technology: Artificial Intelligence and Data Engineering.

Building the pipeline also strengthened my understanding of workflow automation, data quality management, and orchestration concepts. The experience highlighted the importance of reliable data and showed how automation can significantly improve the efficiency of data processing workflows.

Overall, this tutorial expanded my understanding of intelligent data pipelines and provided practical experience with technologies that represent the future direction of data engineering.
