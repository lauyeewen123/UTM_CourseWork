
# 📝 Assignment 1 - Academic Writing

## 📖 Overview

This assignment focuses on academic research and technical writing within the field of Data Engineering. The objective was to investigate an emerging technology trend, conduct a literature review, analyse existing research findings, and present the information in a structured technical report and presentation.

The assignment helped develop research, analytical, and communication skills while improving my understanding of how academic studies contribute to technological advancements.

## 🎯 Learning Outcomes

- Conduct literature reviews using academic sources
- Analyse and evaluate research papers critically

---

## 🔍 Assignment Activities

### Literature Review

Reviewed journal articles, conference papers, and technical publications related to the selected topic. The review focused on understanding current developments, challenges, and future directions within the chosen area of study.

### Technical Report Writing

Prepared a structured academic report that summarizes research findings, compares previous studies, and discusses the significance of the technology in modern applications.

### Presentation

Developed presentation slides to communicate key findings, insights, and conclusions from the literature review.

## 💡 Reflection

This assignment provided valuable exposure to academic research and technical writing practices. Through reviewing multiple journal articles and technical publications, I learned how researchers communicate ideas, evaluate existing work, and identify research gaps.

One of the most beneficial aspects of this assignment was learning how to critically analyse information instead of simply summarising content. I developed stronger research skills by comparing findings from different sources and understanding how technologies evolve through continuous innovation.

Preparing the technical report improved my ability to organise complex information in a structured and professional manner. Meanwhile, the presentation component strengthened my confidence in communicating technical concepts to an audience.

Overall, this assignment enhanced my research methodology, analytical thinking, and technical communication skills, all of which are important for future academic projects and professional work in Data Engineering.
