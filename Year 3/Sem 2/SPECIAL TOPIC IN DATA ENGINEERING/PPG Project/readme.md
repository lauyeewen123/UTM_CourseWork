# 📦 PPG Recoverable Assets & Inventory Risk Management (RA & IRM)

## 📖 Overview

The PPG Recoverable Assets & Inventory Risk Management (RA & IRM) project was developed to support inventory monitoring, risk assessment, and business decision-making through a cloud-based Business Intelligence solution.

The project focuses on identifying inventory risks such as recoverable assets, dead stock, expired materials, stock shortages, and customer impacts. By transforming operational data into analytical datasets and interactive dashboards, stakeholders can gain valuable insights into inventory health and operational performance.

This project integrates data warehousing, cloud analytics, SQL development, and business intelligence technologies to deliver an end-to-end analytical solution.

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Monitor inventory health and financial risks
- Identify materials below reorder level
- Detect Recoverable Assets (RA)
- Identify Dead Stock inventory
- Monitor expired materials
- Calculate inventory provision amounts
- Detect unfulfilled production orders
- Identify customers impacted by stock shortages

---

## 📊 Business Questions Addressed

| Question | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Q1 | Which materials are below reorder level? |
| Q2 | Which materials are classified as Recoverable Assets? |
| Q3 | Which materials are considered Dead Stock? |
| Q4 | Which inventory items have expired? |
| Q5 | What is the total inventory provision amount? |
| Q6 | Which production orders cannot be fulfilled due to stock shortages? |
| Q7 | Which customers are impacted by inventory shortages? |
