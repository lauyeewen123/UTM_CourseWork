## 📚 Key Concepts Learned

### Academic Research

Research articles provide evidence-based knowledge that supports innovation and technological advancement.

### Critical Analysis

Technical publications should be evaluated based on their methodology, findings, limitations, and practical applications.

### Knowledge Discovery

Research helps identify emerging trends, challenges, and opportunities within a field of study.

### Continuous Learning

Keeping up with academic publications is important for staying informed about new developments and industry best practices.

## 💡 Reflection

This activity helped me develop the ability to read and analyse academic literature more effectively. Unlike textbooks or lecture materials, research articles present new findings and innovative approaches that contribute to the advancement of technology.

Through reviewing the selected article, I learned how researchers identify problems, design methodologies, and evaluate results systematically. The experience also improved my ability to critically assess technical information rather than accepting findings at face value.

One of the most valuable lessons from this activity was understanding the importance of continuous learning in the technology industry. As technologies evolve rapidly, professionals must stay updated with new research and emerging trends to remain relevant and informed.

Overall, this tutorial strengthened my research, analytical, and technical communication skills while providing deeper insight into the role of academic research in Data Engineering and Information Technology.
