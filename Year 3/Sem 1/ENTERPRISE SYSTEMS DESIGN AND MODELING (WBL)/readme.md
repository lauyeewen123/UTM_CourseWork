## Reflection

This course provided valuable insights into how enterprise systems support business operations and organizational decision-making. Through assignments, industrial talks, and group activities, I learned how different business functions are integrated through enterprise solutions.

The industrial visits and talks exposed me to real-world enterprise environments and helped me understand how concepts learned in class are applied in industry. These experiences improved my awareness of enterprise technologies and their role in supporting digital transformation.

Overall, this course strengthened my understanding of business processes and enterprise-level system design, which are important aspects of modern information systems.
