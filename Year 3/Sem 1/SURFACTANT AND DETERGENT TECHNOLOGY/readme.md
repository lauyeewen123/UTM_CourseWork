## Reflection

Although this course was different from my core computing subjects, it provided valuable interdisciplinary learning opportunities. Through group projects and research activities, I learned how scientific principles are applied in product formulation and development.

The assignments required extensive research, technical writing, and collaboration, which helped improve my communication and presentation skills. Working with students from different backgrounds also enhanced my teamwork abilities and exposed me to perspectives outside the computing field.

Overall, this course broadened my knowledge beyond technology and demonstrated the importance of interdisciplinary collaboration in solving real-world problems.
