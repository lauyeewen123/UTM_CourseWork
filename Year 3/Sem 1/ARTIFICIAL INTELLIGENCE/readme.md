## Reflection

Artificial Intelligence introduced me to the principles behind intelligent systems and how computers can be designed to simulate human reasoning and decision-making. Through assignments and the AI Triage Assistant project, I learned how AI techniques can be applied to solve practical problems.

This course broadened my understanding of emerging technologies and their impact on industries such as healthcare, business, and data analytics. It also strengthened my critical thinking and problem-solving skills by encouraging me to analyze complex scenarios and design intelligent solutions.

Overall, this course increased my interest in machine learning and AI-driven technologies, which are becoming increasingly important in the field of data engineering.
