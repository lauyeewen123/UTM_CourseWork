## Reflection

This course marked my first exposure to data analysis and statistics. Through assignments and projects, I learned how to collect, analyse, and interpret data using statistical techniques. The project allowed me to apply concepts such as probability distributions, hypothesis testing, and data visualization to real datasets.

The knowledge gained from this course became an important foundation for later subjects such as Data Mining, Data Analytics Programming, Business Intelligence, and Data Engineering. It also improved my analytical thinking and ability to make evidence-based decisions.
