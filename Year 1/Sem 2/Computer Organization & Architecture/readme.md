## Reflection

This course gave me a deeper understanding of how computers operate internally. Learning assembly language helped me appreciate how high-level programs are translated into machine-level instructions. Through laboratory exercises and projects, I gained practical experience working directly with processor instructions, registers, and memory management concepts.

One of the most valuable lessons from this course was understanding the relationship between hardware and software. Although assembly programming was challenging, it strengthened my logical thinking and debugging skills. Overall, this course provided a strong foundation for future studies in operating systems and software development.
