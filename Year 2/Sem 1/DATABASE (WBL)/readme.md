## Reflection

Database was one of the most important courses in my academic journey because it introduced the foundations of data management and database design. Through the Technicrab project, I learned how to identify entities, relationships, and business rules before translating them into a structured database model.

Creating the ERD, normalization exercises, and design reports helped me understand how databases support real business operations. This course became the foundation for my later interests in data engineering, business intelligence, and database programming.
